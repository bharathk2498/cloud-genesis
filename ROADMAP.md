# Roadmap

This roadmap outlines current areas of focus and planned features for
future releases of **Cloud Genesis**.  It is not an exhaustive list
but should provide a sense of direction.  Contributions and
suggestions are welcome—open an issue or a discussion to propose
changes.

## Near term

- **Additional cloud adapters** – Extend support to OpenStack and
  VMware Cloud on AWS.
- **Improved data migration** – Integrate additional CDC engines and
  schema evolution tooling.
- **Automated landing zone deployment** – Provide turnkey scripts to
  deploy secure, compliant landing zones in minutes.
- **Enhanced FinOps models** – Incorporate machine learning models to
  detect cost anomalies in real‑time.
- **Expanded AI copilot capabilities** – Enable natural language
  queries against the configuration and runbooks.

## Mid term

- **Plugins for Kubernetes and serverless** – Add discovery and
  migration support for containerised and serverless workloads.
- **Cross‑account / cross‑project migrations** – Automate moving
  workloads between accounts/projects within the same provider.
- **Multi‑tenant operation** – Provide isolation and governance for
  large enterprises with multiple business units.
- **Compliance pack templates** – Deliver pre‑packaged policy sets for
  regulatory frameworks such as PCI DSS, HIPAA and GDPR.

## Long term

- **Graph visualisation** – Visualise dependencies and migration
  waves in an interactive graph UI.
- **Dynamic scheduling** – Use AI to adapt migration waves based on
  real‑time utilisation and risk factors.
- **Community ecosystem** – Encourage community‑contributed adapters,
  policies and dashboards; provide a marketplace for sharing modules.
